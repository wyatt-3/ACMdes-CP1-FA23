Week 5 Reading Quotes


"If graphic designers do not become fluent in this new digital reality, they will become irrelevant.""

This one really spoke out to me. It's why I decided to get my Masters in Interaction design. I used to come from a design background specializing in marketing and coming up with ideas. Concepting is a timeless skill, but I needed to gain some more tangible skills to increase my creative range.

“We are now in transition from an object-oriented to a systems-oriented culture. Here change emanates, not from things, but from the way things are done.”

As someone who's big into crypto & web3, I couldn't agree more. People that don't understand NFTs just haven't woken up to the new system.

"We now spend a majority of our time looking at screens instead of paper, and this has created a great need for designers who understand how to design for digital devices."

I believe digital art in the future will be just as coveted as paper art we all uninimously agree is "art"



"Wanting to work in the intersection between art and code."

Coding has always fascinated me. Maybe the grass looks greener on the other side, but as a fine arts major this was something I always wished I was good at. Making apps, websites, and even smart contracts would be a dream skillset of mine.

"The pizza box was a very efficient prototype."

This sentence made me smile. It shows sometimes you can't over look the simple things. And just because it's simple, doesn't mean it's ineffective. Work smarter not harder.

"Interactive computer systems are complex.""

This is a very underrated sentence. As someone who uses a computer everyday and it still is a magic box to me.
